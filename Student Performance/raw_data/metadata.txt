Dataset Name: Student Performance Dataset

Description:
This dataset contains the performance scores of students in three subjects: math, reading, and writing. Additionally, it includes demographic and social factors such as gender, ethnicity, parental education level, lunch type, and whether the student completed a test preparation course.

This dataset is valuable for analyzing how factors like gender, parental education, and test preparation impact student performance.



Dataset Structure:

- gender: Categorical  
  - Student's gender: 'male' or 'female'. Represents the student's gender.

- race/ethnicity: Categorical  
  - Ethnicity group of the student.

- parental level of education: Categorical  
  - Highest level of education attained by the student's parents.

- lunch: Categorical  
  - Type of lunch: 'standard' or 'free/reduced'. Indicates whether the student receives a free or reduced-price lunch.

- test preparation course: Categorical  
  - Whether the student completed a test preparation course: 'completed' or 'none'.

- math score: Numerical (0-100)  
  - The student's score in math.

- reading score: Numerical (0-100)  
  - The student's score in reading.

- writing score: Numerical (0-100)  
  - The student's score in writing.



Important Notes:

1. Gender Encoding: In the dataset, 'male' is represented as 0 and 'female' as 1.
2. Numerical Scores: The math, reading, and writing scores are numeric and are on a scale from 0 to 100.
